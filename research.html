Skyrmion Logic

Skyrmions are topological quasiparticles known to exist in ferromagnetic thin films with high Dzyaloshinsky–Moriya interaction. Through my work, I've improved the efficiency and accuracy of these logic schemes. Please find below multiple videos demonstrating the operation of skyrmion logic circuits.
